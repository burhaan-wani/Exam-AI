Course objectives: 
1. Understand the Fundamentals of Generative AI. 
2. Master the Mathematics and Theory Behind Generative models. 
3. Develop Practical Skills in Implementing Generative models. 
4. Use appropriate metrics to evaluate the performance of Generative models. 
Course Outcomes [CO]: At the end of the course, student will be able to:
CO1 
Articulate the fundamental principles and objectives of generative models
CO2 
Utilize probability theory and statistical methods to understand and develop generative models
CO3 
Implement Generative Models
CO4 
Evaluate Generative Model Performance
